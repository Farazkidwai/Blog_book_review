Personality By Daniel Nettle




Daniel Nettle is reader of psychology and anthropology at the university of Newcastle, He has written books on many aspects of human life like Happiness, Strong Imagination and vanishing voices

Book review


In this book Daniel Nettle discusses about five personality types discussed in five factor model by Francis Galton, namely

  1. Extraversion
  2. Neuroticism
  3. Conscientiousness
  4. Agreebealness
  5. Openess

Author also talks about how the genetics affect the personality types.
Each Chapter is dedicated to each personality type, where the author discusses about the characteristics of each personality type and also debates about the advanatges and disadvantages of each personality types or fellings associated with each personality type.
At the end of the book he gives an assesment developed at Newcastle university, this assesment (according to him) can be used to determine the personality type of a person. You can try it here.
